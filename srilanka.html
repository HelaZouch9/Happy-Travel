sirilanka

why choosing sirilanka:

Sri Lanka, an enchanting island nation nestled in the Indian Ocean, captivates travelers with its unique blend of natural beauty, rich history, and diverse culture. Colombo, the bustling capital, is a vibrant metropolis showcasing a harmonious fusion of modernity and tradition. The country's ancient cities, such as Anuradhapura and Polonnaruwa, boast impressive archaeological sites, including sacred temples and ancient stupas, providing a glimpse into Sri Lanka's storied past.

travel itinerary

Embark on an enchanting odyssey with our agency, where we present a meticulously curated journey across the captivating landscapes of Sri Lanka, known as Sirilanka. In this thoughtfully organized itinerary spanning seven days, we seamlessly blend the country's rich natural beauty, profound historical sites, captivating museums, lively nightlife, and delectable culinary experiences

Day 1: Exploring Colombo and Surroundings

  1. Morning (9:00 AM - 1:00 PM): Arrival and transfer to the hotel, followed by a visit to the Gangaramaya Temple and Independence Memorial Hall .
  2. Afternoon (2:00 PM - 5:00 PM): Explore Viharamahadevi Park and the Galle Face Green Promenade .
  3. Evening (7:00 PM - 9:00 PM): Enjoy dinner at a local seafood restaurant .

Day 2: Cultural Triangle - Sigiriya and Dambulla

  1. Morning (8:00 AM - 1:00 PM): Travel to Sigiriya and climb the Sigiriya Rock Fortress .
  2. Afternoon (2:00 PM - 5:00 PM): Visit the Dambulla Cave Temple .
  3. Evening (7:00 PM - 9:00 PM): Relax at your accommodation or explore the local surroundings.

Day 3: Kandy - Hill Country Charm

  1. Morning (9:00 AM - 12:00 PM): Travel to Kandy and visit the Temple of the Tooth .
  2. Afternoon (1:00 PM - 4:00 PM): Explore the Peradeniya Royal Botanical Gardens .
  3. Evening (7:00 PM - 9:00 PM): Attend a cultural dance performance .

Day 4: Nuwara Eliya - Tea Country Serenity

  1. Morning (8:00 AM - 12:00 PM): Journey to Nuwara Eliya and visit a tea plantation .
  2. Afternoon (1:00 PM - 4:00 PM): Explore Gregory Lake and enjoy a boat ride.
  3. Evening (7:00 PM - 9:00 PM): Stroll through the picturesque town .

Day 5: Yala National Park - Wildlife Safari

  1. Morning (6:00 AM - 12:00 PM): Travel to Yala and enjoy a morning safari in Yala National Park .
  2. Afternoon (1:00 PM - 4:00 PM): Relax or opt for an optional evening safari .
  3. Evening (8:00 PM - 10:00 PM): Dinner at the accommodation.

Day 6: Galle - Coastal Beauty

  1. Morning (9:00 AM - 12:00 PM): Head to Galle and explore the Galle Fort .
  2. Afternoon (1:00 PM - 4:00 PM): Visit the Maritime Museum and enjoy some free time.
  3. Evening (7:00 PM - 9:00 PM): Dinner in a seaside restaurant.

Day 7: Departure

  1. Morning (9:00 AM - 12:00 PM): Leisure time or last-minute shopping
  2. Afternoon: Check-out and departure

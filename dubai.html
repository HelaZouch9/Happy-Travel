dubai

why choosing dubai:

Dubai, a dynamic city in the United Arab Emirates, stands as a testament to modern luxury and innovation. Nestled on the southeastern coast of the Arabian Peninsula, Dubai has rapidly transformed into a global hub, captivating tourists with its futuristic skyline and opulent lifestyle. The city is renowned for iconic landmarks such as the Burj Khalifa, the world's tallest skyscraper, and the Palm Jumeirah, an artificial archipelago shaped like a palm tree. Dubai's cosmopolitan atmosphere is epitomized by its diverse population and a bustling blend of cultures. The city's commitment to architectural excellence is evident in structures like the Burj Al Arab, a sail-shaped hotel synonymous with luxury and extravagance. Shopping enthusiasts flock to Dubai's extravagant malls and traditional souks, where everything from haute couture to traditional spices is on display.

travel itinerary

Embark on an extraordinary voyage with our agency as we unveil a meticulously curated journey through the captivating city of Dubai. Across seven days, our thoughtfully crafted itinerary seamlessly weaves together the best of nature, history, avant-garde museums, pulsating nightlife, and exquisite culinary adventures

Day 1: Arrival in Dubai - A Glittering Welcome

  1. Morning (10:00 AM - 1:00 PM): Arrival and check-in to your luxurious hotel.
  2. Afternoon (2:00 PM - 5:00 PM): Explore the iconic Burj Khalifa and take in panoramic views of the city.
  3. Evening (7:00 PM - 9:00 PM): Dinner at a rooftop restaurant overlooking the Dubai Fountain.

Day 2: Dubai's Modern Wonders

  1. Morning (9:00 AM - 12:00 PM): Visit the Dubai Mall and experience the mesmerizing Dubai Aquarium.
  2. Afternoon (1:00 PM - 3:00 PM): Take a stroll in the Miracle Garden, a floral paradise in the desert.
  3. Evening (6:00 PM - 8:00 PM): Enjoy an optional desert safari with a traditional Arabian dinner.

Day 3: Old Dubai - Heritage and Culture

  1. Morning (10:00 AM - 1:00 PM): Explore the historic Al Fahidi District and Dubai Museum.
  2. Afternoon (2:00 PM - 4:00 PM): Abra ride across Dubai Creek to the bustling Gold and Spice Souks.
  3. Evening (7:00 PM - 9:00 PM): Dinner in a traditional Arabic restaurant in the Al Bastakiya Quarter.

Day 4: Beach Day and Luxury Shopping

  1. Morning (9:00 AM - 12:00 PM): Relax at Jumeirah Beach and take a dip in the warm waters.
  2. Afternoon (1:00 PM - 4:00 PM): Indulge in luxury shopping at the Mall of the Emirates .
  3. Evening (7:00 PM - 9:00 PM): Dinner at Pierchic, an overwater restaurant with stunning views.

Day 5: Adventure and Entertainment

  1. Morning (10:00 AM - 1:00 PM): Experience indoor skiing at Ski Dubai in the Mall of the Emirates.
  2. Afternoon (2:00 PM - 5:00 PM): Visit IMG Worlds of Adventure, one of the world's largest indoor theme parks.
  3. Evening (8:00 PM - 10:00 PM): Attend a mesmerizing performance at La Perle by Dragone.

Day 6: Day Trip to Abu Dhabi - Capital Extravaganza

  1. Morning (9:00 AM - 12:00 PM): Travel to Abu Dhabi and visit the Sheikh Zayed Grand Mosque.
  2. Afternoon (1:00 PM - 4:00 PM): Explore the Louvre Abu Dhabi and the Emirates Palace.
  3. Evening (6:00 PM - 8:00 PM): Return to Dubai and have dinner at Pier 7 with a view of the city skyline.

Day 7: Departure

  1. Morning (10:00 AM - 12:00 PM): Leisure time or last-minute shopping
  2. Afternoon: Check-out and departure
